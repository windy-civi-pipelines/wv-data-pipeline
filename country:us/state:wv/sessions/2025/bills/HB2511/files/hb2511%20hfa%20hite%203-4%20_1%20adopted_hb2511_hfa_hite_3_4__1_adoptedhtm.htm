Delegate Hite moved to amend the bill on page 3, line 49, by striking the words, “a Veteran Service Organization, pursuant to §60-7-12(a)(2) et seq.” and inserting in lieu thereof the following, “a fraternal society or veterans’ organization that is: (A) A fraternal beneficiary society that is exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(8) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended; (B) a domestic fraternal society that is exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(10) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended; (C) a veterans’ organization that is exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(19) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended or (D) a volunteer fire department, as defined in §11-13JJ-2.”
