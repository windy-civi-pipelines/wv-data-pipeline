HB2042 S JUD AMT #1 3-10

Curia  

The Committee on the Judiciary moved to amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting therefor a new title, to read as follows:

Eng. Com. Sub. for House Bill 2042 — A Bill to amend §49-4-601 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, relating to procedures in cases of child neglect or abuse; allowing a prosecuting attorney on behalf of the Department of Human Services, attorneys for a parent, or a guardian ad litem to file a petition requesting the appointment of a court appointed special advocate if that circuit court is serviced by a court appointed special advocate.
