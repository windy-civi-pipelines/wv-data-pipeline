Red = new code to be enacted Committee SubstituteforHOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 12(By Delegate Sheedy)[Originating in the Committee on Energy and Public Works; Reported on March 10, 2025] Requesting the Division of Highways name Bridge Number: 52-013/00-004.64  () (52A039), (39.67254, -80.45228) locally known as Hundred Covered Bridge, carrying CR 013 over WV Fork Fish Creek in Wetzel County, the "PFC Charles 'Charlie' Goff Memorial Bridge".Whereas, Charles "Charlie" Goff lived a life of distinction worthy of remembrance; andWhereas, Charlie was born on March 12, 1933; and Whereas, Charlie was a born and bred West Virginian who hailed from Rowlesburg in Preston County; andWhereas, Charlie served his country well as soldier in the United States Army and achieved the rank of Private First Class; andWhereas, PFC Goff’s most significant duty assignment was as a member of C Company, 15th Tank Battalion, 6th Armored Division; andWhereas, PFC Goff served until he was honorably discharged from the Army; andWhereas, PFC Goff passed away on August 24th, 2024, to the great sadness of his family and friends; therefore, be it Resolved by the Legislature of West Virginia:That the Division of Highways name Bridge Number: 52-013/00-004.64  () (52A039), (39.67254, -80.45228) locally known as Hundred Covered Bridge, carrying CR 013 over WV Fork Fish Creek in Wetzel County, the "PFC Charles "Charlie" Goff Memorial Bridge," and, be it Further Resolved, That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to have made and be placed signs identifying the bridge as the "PFC Charles 'Charlie' Goff Memorial Bridge"; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk of the House forward a copy of this resolution to the Commissioner of the Division of Highways.
