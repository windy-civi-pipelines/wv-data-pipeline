Red = new code to be enacted HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 15(By Delegate Steele)[Introduced February 13, 2025; Referred to the Committee on Energy and Public Works then Rules.]Requesting the Division of Highways name Bridge Number: 41-077/00-028.93  (NB) (41A210), (37.61525, -81.11937) locally known as I-77 NB OVER RAL 48, carrying IS 077 over CR 48 in Raleigh County, the "U.S. Marines PFC Delbert Carles Roles Memorial Bridge."Whereas, Delbert Carles Roles lived a life worthy of remembrance; and Whereas, Delbert was born on November 17th, 1921, in Odd, WV; andWhereas, Delbert enlisted the United States Marines on February 6th, 1942, and, two years later, found himself abord the LVT #269 sailing to Guam; andWhereas, Once in Guam, Delbert fought in the Second Battle of Guam in a fight that lasted from July 21st to August 10th, 1944, and featured some of the fiercest confrontations of World War 2; andWhereas, After surviving the initial landing, which included fierce fighting and the loss of many soldiers, Delbert continued honoring his fellow soldiers with vigilant service until he was killed in action on July 29th, 1944; andWhereas, PFC Delbert C. Roles was buried in AN&M Cemetery #1, Grave #5, Row #13 at Asan Point, Guam, Marian Islands; andWhereas, Five years later, on January 26th, 1949, Delbert's father requested that Delbert be reburied in the National Memorial Cemetery of the Pacific and he was laid to rest in Section C, Site 1271 in Honolulu, Hawaii where he remains today; therefore, be itResolved by the Legislature of West Virginia:That the Division of Highways name Bridge Number: 41-077/00-043.59  (SB) (41A229), (37.78047, -81.22121) locally known as I-77 OVER CABELL RD, carrying IS 077 over WHITESTICK CRK/CABELL RD in Raleigh County, the "U.S. Marines PFC Delbert Carles Roles Memorial Bridge" and, be it Further Resolved, That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to have made and be placed signs identifying the bridge as the "U.S. Marines PFC Delbert Carles Roles Memorial Bridge," and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk of the House forward a copy of this resolution to the Commissioner of the Division of Highways.
