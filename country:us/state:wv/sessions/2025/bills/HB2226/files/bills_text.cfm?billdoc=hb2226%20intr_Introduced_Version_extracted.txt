SmithIntroduced February 12, 2025; referred to the Committee on Government OrganizationA BILL to amend and reenact §64-2-1 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, relating to authorizing the Information Services and Communications Divisions to promulgate a legislative rule relating to telecommunications payments by spending units.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 2.
