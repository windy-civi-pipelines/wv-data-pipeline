Red = new code to be enacted SENATE RESOLUTION 2(By Senator Barrett)[Introduced January 8, 2024] Authorizing the appointment of permanent and per diem employees for the First Regular Session of the Eighty-Seventh Legislature and payment of their compensation.Resolved by the Senate:That the Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate is hereby authorized to appoint employees to receive the per diem compensation, as herein provided, during this regular sixty-day session of the Eighty-Seventh Legislature, and any extension thereof as follows:Up to thirty Class I administrative assistants at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to one hundred thirty dollars per diem;Up to four Class II administrative assistants at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to one hundred forty dollars per diem;Up to five legislative analysts at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to two hundred ten dollars per diem;Up to ten legal counselors at a rate of two hundred fifty dollars per diem to four hundred dollars per diem; assignments and salary to be determined by the presiding officer of the Senate;One Senior Advisor at a rate of three hundred dollars per diem;One Bill Clerk at a rate of ninety dollars per diem to one hundred dollars per diem;Up to four proofreaders at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to one hundred thirty dollars per diem;One Page Program Coordinator at a rate of one hundred fifteen dollars per diem to one hundred fifty dollars per diem;One Sergeant at Arms at a rate to be determined by the presiding officer of the Senate;Up to two assistants to the Sergeant at Arms at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to one hundred twenty dollars per diem;One Doorkeeper at a rate to be determined by the presiding officer of the Senate;Up to six assistants to the Doorkeeper at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to one hundred twenty dollars per diem;One Supervisor for Materials and Supplies at a rate of one hundred forty five dollars per diemUp to three mail clerks at a rate of one hundred dollars per diem to one hundred twenty dollars per diem;The Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate shall have authority to employ such per diem staff personnel during any session of the Legislature as shall be needed in addition to staff personnel authorized by this Senate resolution adopted during any such session.  The Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate shall have authority to employ such staff personnel between sessions of the Legislature as shall be needed, the compensation of all staff personnel during and between sessions of the Legislature, notwithstanding any such Senate resolution, to be fixed by the presiding officer of the Senate.  In all such cases, the Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate shall have authority to supervise and dismiss such staff personnel; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the appointment of salaried full-time employees of the Senate for the year two thousand twenty and their compensation, at the following amounts per month, for such periods of time as they are employed in the positions designated, is hereby authorized, as follows:Clerk									9,620.83Chief of Staff							          11,908.33Assistant Clerk							7,750.77Executive Assistant to Minor Committees						5,443.33Executive Assistant to President					4,370.83Director of Communications						8,575.00Parliamentarian							6,998.33Counsel to Minority Leader						8,500.00Administrative Assistant to Minority Leader				5,833.33Counsel to Education Committee					9,203.33Clerk/Analyst to Education Committee				4,908.33Counsel to Finance Committee					9,568.33Budget Policy Analyst to Finance Committee			7,370.83Assistant to Finance Chairman/Clerk					5,704.17Counsel to Government Organization				9,276.67Clerk to Government Organization Committee			5,526.67Admin. Assistant to Government Organization Committee		5,704.17Counsel to Health and Human Resources Committee		8,860.00Clerk to Health and Human Resources Committee			5,741.67Counsel to Judiciary Committee					8,943.33Clerk/Analyst to Judiciary Committee					5,704.17Deputy Chief of Staff							6,735.00Mail Clerk								4,165.00Custodian								2,944.52The Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate shall have authority to employ such full-time staff personnel during any session of the Legislature as shall be needed in addition to staff personnel authorized by this Senate resolution adopted during any such session.  The Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate shall have authority to employ such staff personnel between sessions of the Legislature as shall be needed, the compensation of all staff personnel during and between sessions of the Legislature, notwithstanding any such Senate resolution, to be fixed by the presiding officer of the Senate.  In all such cases, the Clerk of the Senate, with the approval of the presiding officer of the Senate, or the presiding officer of the Senate shall have authority to supervise and dismiss such staff personnel.
