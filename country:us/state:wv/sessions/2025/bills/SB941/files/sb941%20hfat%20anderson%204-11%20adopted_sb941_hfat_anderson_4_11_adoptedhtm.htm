Delegate Anderson moved to amend Senate Bill 941 by striking out the title and substituting therefor a new title, to read as follows:

Senate Bill 941 – “A BILL to amend and reenact §19-21A-8 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, relating to clarifying certain authority regarding dams owned or sponsored by local conservation districts; providing that any alteration, improvement, or agreement related to a dam owned or sponsored by a local conservation district is subject solely to the authority of the Department of Environmental Protection; and providing that the provisions of this subsection may not be construed to affect or alter any state or federal funding to the West Virginia Conservation Agency.”
