Red = new code to be enacted West Virginia Legislature2025 REGULAR SESSIONIntroductionSenate Bill 412By Senator Woodrum[Introduced February 13, 2025;  referred to the Committee on the Judiciary]A BILL to amend and reenact §64-10-1 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, relating to authorizing the Division of Natural Resources to promulgate a legislative rule relating to transporting and selling wildlife parts and pelts.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 10.
