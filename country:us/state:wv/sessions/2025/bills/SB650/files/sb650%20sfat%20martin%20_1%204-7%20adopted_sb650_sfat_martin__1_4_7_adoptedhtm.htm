Senator Martin moved to amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting therefor a new title, to read as follows:

Eng. Senate Bill 650—A BILL to amend and reenact §18-5-18a of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, relating to providing that a full-time interventionist hired and assigned to up to two classrooms satisfies the early childhood assistant teacher, aide, or paraprofessional related requirements for kindergarten through third grades for both classrooms; excluding kindergarten and first grade from authority to cover two classrooms with one full-time interventionist; providing that any early childhood assistant teacher, aide, paraprofessional, or interventionist assigned to a kindergarten or first grade classroom must be full-time in order to satisfy certain requirements; and clarifying that a part-time interventionist only satisfies those requirements for one classroom.
