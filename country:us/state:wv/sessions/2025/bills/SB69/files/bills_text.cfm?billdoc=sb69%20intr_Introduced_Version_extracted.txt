Red = new code to be enacted WEST VIRGINIA LEGISLATURE2025 REGULAR SESSIONIntroducedSenate Bill 69By Senator Maynard[Introduced February 12, 2025; referredto the Committee on Health and Human Resources]A BILL to amend the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, by adding a new article, designated §16-2S-1, relating to requiring physicians to notify parents when prescribing contraceptives to minors; and providing definitions.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 2S. PARENTAL NOTIFICATION OF PRESCRIBED CONTRACEPTIVES. §16-2S-1. Contraceptives; parental notice; definitions. (a) A physician located within this state shall provide parental notification when prescribing a contraceptive to a minor.(b) For the purposes of this section:(1) "Contraceptive" means a drug or device approved by the Food and Drug Administration to prevent pregnancy.(2) "Minor" means a person under the age of 18 years who has not graduated from high school. NOTE: The purpose of this bill is to require physicians to notify parents when prescribing contraceptives to minors.
