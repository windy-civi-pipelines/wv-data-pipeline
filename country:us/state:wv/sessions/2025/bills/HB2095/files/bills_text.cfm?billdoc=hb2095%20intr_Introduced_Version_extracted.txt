Red = new code to be enacted WEST VIRGINIA LEGISLATURE2025 REGULAR SESSIONIntroducedHouse Bill 2095By Delegate G. Howell[Introduced February 12, 2025; referredto the Committee on Energy and Public Works]A BILL to amend the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, by adding thereto a new section, designated §17-30-2, relating to requiring that new electric vehicle charging stations are universally accessible.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: Article 30. Electric Vehicle Economic Development Plan. §17-30-2. Electric vehicle charging stations shall be universal. (a) Any new vehicle charging stations for electric or plug in hybrid vehicles installed in the state of West Virginia for public use, after January 1, 2026, must be universal and be able to charge any vehicle manufactured after, July 1, 2025, by the following manufacturers:(1) General Motors (Chevrolet, GMC, Cadillac, Buick) (2) Ford Motor Company (Ford, Lincoln) (3) Toyota Motor Corporation (Toyota, Lexus) (4) Stellantis (formerly Fiat Chrysler Automobiles) (Jeep, Ram, Dodge, Chrysler, Fiat)  (5) Honda Motor Co., Ltd. (Honda, Acura) (6) Hyundai Motor Group (Hyundai, Kia) (7) Nissan Motor Co., Ltd. (Nissan, Infiniti) (8) Subaru Corporation (Subaru) (9) Volkswagen Group (Volkswagen, Audi, Porsche) (10) BMW Group (BMW, Mini) (11) Mercedes-Benz USA (Mercedes-Benz, smart) (12) Mazda Motor Corporation (Mazda) (13) Volvo Car Group (Volvo) (14) Jaguar Land Rover Automotive (Jaguar, Land Rover) (15) Mitsubishi Motors Corporation (Mitsubishi) (16) Tesla, Inc. (Tesla) Genesis Motor (Genesis) (17) Porsche Cars North America (Porsche) (18) Genesis Motor (Genesis).(b) The Legislature finds that universal electric vehicle charging stations are like standardized fuel nozzles at gas stations. Universal electric vehicle charging stations are essential, ensuring that, like all gas stations accommodating every gasoline car, they can refuel all electric vehicle makes and models efficiently, regardless of their manufacturer. This will make West Virginia a better place for our citizens with electric vehicles. NOTE: The purpose of this bill is to require that new electric vehicle charging stations be universally accessible.Strike-throughs indicate language that would be stricken from a heading or the present law and underscoring indicates new language that would be added.
