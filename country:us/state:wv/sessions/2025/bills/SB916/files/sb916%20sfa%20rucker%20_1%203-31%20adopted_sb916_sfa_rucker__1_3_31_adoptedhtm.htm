SB916 SFA Rucker #1 3-31

Kraus  7502

 

Senator Rucker moved to amend the bill on page 1, section 7, lines 4 - 31, by striking out the remainder of the bill inserting in lieu thereof the following:

(b) A county commission, municipality, or public health department may not adopt or enact an ordinance, rule, license requirement, or other authorization that contravenes or is stricter than any state law, rule, or regulation relating to agricultural operations, as defined in §19-19-2 of this code. Any ordinance, rule, regulation, license requirement, or other authorization previously adopted by a county commission, municipality, or public health department that contravenes or is stricter than any state law, rule, or regulation regarding agricultural operations is revoked.
