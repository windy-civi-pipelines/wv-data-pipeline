Delegate Fehrenbacher moves to amend the committee substitute bill on page 2, section 21, after line 29, by inserting a new subdivision (8) to read as follows:

“(8) The amendments to §5B-2-21(b)(6), (d), (e), (g), and (j) of this code enacted during the regular session of the Legislature, 2025, shall not impact any microgrid district that was previously certified on or before January 1, 2024, or any special contract entered into and approved by the Public Service Commission on or before January 1, 2025. No amendments to this section enacted during the regular session of the Legislature, 2025, shall be interpreted to remove an existing microgrid district certification.”
