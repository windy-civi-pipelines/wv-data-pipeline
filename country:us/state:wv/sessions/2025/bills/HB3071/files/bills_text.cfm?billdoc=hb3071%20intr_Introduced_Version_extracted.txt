Red = new code to be enacted WEST virginia legislature2025 regular session FISCAL NOTE IntroducedHouse Bill 3071By Delegates Hillenbrand, Mazzocchi, Stephens, Drennan, and Ward[Introduced; referred to the Committee on]A BILL to amend the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, by adding a new section, designated §21-3A-10, relating to requiring an opioid antagonist be included in workplace first-aid kits.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: Article 3A Occupational Safety and Health Act. §21-3A-10. Ensuring access to naloxone or opioid antagonist in the workplace. The Commissioner or his or her agent shall, before December 1, 2027, require and ensure that first aid materials in a workplace include naloxone hydrochloride or another opioid antagonist approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration to reverse opioid overdose and instructions for using the opioid antagonist.NOTE: The purpose of this bill is to require an opioid antagonist be included in workplace first-aid kits.Strike-throughs indicate language that would be stricken from a heading or the present law and underscoring indicates new language that would be added.
