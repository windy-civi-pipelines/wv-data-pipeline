Red = new code to be enacted SENATE RESOLUTION 8(By Senators Morris, Hamilton, and Jeffries)[Introduced February 17, 2025] Designating February 18, 2025, as West Virginia Banker’s Day at the Legislature.Whereas, The banking industry provides essential capital, support, and resources to West Virginia’s citizens, businesses, and governments and is an economic driver for the state of West Virginia; andWhereas, A robust banking industry supports increased economic development and prosperity for West Virginia in every sector, from manufacturing to health care to small retail businesses; and Whereas, The banking industry plays an essential role in new economic development initiatives for West Virginia, creating jobs and diversifying West Virginia’s economy; and Whereas, A robust and well-supported banking industry positions West Virginia’s residents to achieve greater financial success through home ownership, small business support, investment advice, and other services; andWhereas, According to the American Bankers Association, the banking industry employs nearly 8,000 West Virginians with an average salary twice the state average and is present and accessible in nearly every community; andWhereas, There are 44 banks headquartered in West Virginia and over 570 branch banks in our communities; andWhereas, The banking industry provides over $2 billion in annual funding to West Virginia businesses through nearly 73,000 business loans; andWhereas, The West Virginia Bankers Association proudly works to support and enhance West Virginia’s banking climate in a new era of economic prosperity for our state; therefore, be itResolved by the Senate:That the Senate hereby designates February 18, 2025, as West Virginia Banker’s Day at the Legislature; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk is hereby directed to forward a copy of this resolution to the appropriate representative at the West Virginia Banker’s Association.
