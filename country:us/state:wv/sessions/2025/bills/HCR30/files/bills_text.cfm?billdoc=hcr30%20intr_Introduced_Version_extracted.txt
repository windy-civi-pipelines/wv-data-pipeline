Red = new code to be enacted HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 30(By Delegates Toney and Vance)[Introduced February 14, 2025; Referredto the committee on Energy and Public Works then Rules ]Requesting the Division of Highways name Bridge Number: 55-009/04-006.66  () (55A028), (37.66467, -81.60096) locally known as COON BRANCH BRIDGE, carrying CR 009/04 over LAUREL FORK in Wyoming County, the "Pastor Roger D. Stewart and Brenda Runion Stewart Memorial Bridge."Whereas, Pastor Roger D. Stewart lived a life worthy of remembrance;Whereas, Pastor Stewart was born on October 19th, 1946, in Matheny, WV and passed away on April 30th, 2018; andWhereas, Pastor Stewart was the child of French and Lettie Stewart;Whereas, Pastor Stewart had a long career as a Pastor, serving in many churches, before retiring as the Pastor of the Campus Church of God in Cyclone, WV; andWhereas, Pastor Stewart enjoyed singing with the Harless Family, evangelizing, and visiting with family and friends; andWhereas, Pastor Stewart is survived by his wife and partner in ministry Brenda Runion Stewart, son Mark (Cecilia) Stewart, daughter Kim (Shawn) Sanders, seventeen grandchildren, and numerous other family and friends; therefore, be it Resolved by the Legislature of West Virginia:That the Division of Highways name Bridge Number: 55-009/04-006.66  () (55A028), (37.66467, -81.60096) locally known as COON BRANCH BRIDGE, carrying CR 009/04 over LAUREL FORK in Wyoming County, the "Pastor Roger D. Stewart and Brenda Runion Stewart Memorial Bridge," and, be it Further Resolved, That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to have made and be placed signs identifying the bridge as the "Pastor Roger D. Stewart and Brenda Runion Stewart Memorial Bridge"; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk of the House forward a copy of this resolution to the Commissioner of the Division of Highways.
