Red = new code to be enacted WEST VIRGINIA LEGISLATURE2025 REGULAR SESSIONIntroducedSenate Bill 744By Senator Rucker[Introduced March 12, 2025; referredto the Committee on Government Organization]A BILL to repeal §5A-3A-1, §5A-3A-2, §5A-3A-3, §5A-3A-4, §5A-3A-5, and §5A-3A-6 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, relating to the purchase of commodities and services from the handicapped.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 3A. CENTRAL NONPROFIT COORDINATING AGENCY AND COMMITTEE FOR THE PURCHASE OF COMMODITIES AND SERVICES FROM THE HANDICAPPED. §1. Repeal of article creating the Central Nonprofit Coordinating Agency and Committee for the Purchase of Commodities and Services from the Handicapped. That §5A-3A-1, §5A-3A-2, §5A-3A-3, §5A-3A-4, §5A-3A-5, and §5A-3A-6 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, are repealed. NOTE: The purpose of this bill is to remove requirements for purchase of certain commodities and services and terminate the Committee for the Purchase of Commodities and services from the Handicapped.Strike-throughs indicate language that would be stricken from a heading or the present law and underscoring indicates new language that would be added.
