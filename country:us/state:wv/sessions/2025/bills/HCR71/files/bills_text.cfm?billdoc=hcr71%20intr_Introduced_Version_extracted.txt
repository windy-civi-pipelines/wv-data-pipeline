Red = new code to be enacted HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 71(By Delegates Moore, Dittman, Drennan, Crouse, White, and Petitto)[March 4, 2025; Referred to the Committee on Energy and Public Worksthen Rules ] Requesting the Division of Highways name bridge number 44-010/00-001.09 (44A024), locally known as MT OLIVE BRIDGE (SCBB), beginning at Latitude 38.815807, Longitude ‑81.430185, ending at Latitude 38.815550, Longitude -81.430992, carrying Reedyville Road, CR 10, over Left Fork Reedy Creek in Roane County, the "U. S. Army Technician Fifth Grade Leonard N. Miller Memorial Bridge".Whereas, Leonard N. Miller was born on June 8, 1926, in Spencer, WV to Lemuel Newton and Flora Nester Miller; andWhereas, Leonard enlisted as a dental assistant in the United States Army on January 30, 1945; andWhereas, Leonard was assigned to the Medical Detachment for the 188th Parachute Infantry Battalion, 11th Airborne Division; andWhereas, Leonard was awarded the Asiatic Pacific Service Medal, Good Conduct Medal, World War II Victory Medal, and the Airborne Parachutist Badge; andWhereas, Leonard was honorably discharged from the service on November 1, 1946; andWhereas, Following his military service, Leonard returned to West Virginia, retired from Pennzoil, and worked as a mechanic at the International Garage. He enjoyed farming and collecting firearms; andWhereas, Leonard died on December 14, 2017; andWhereas, It is fitting that an enduring memorial be established to commemorate U.S. Army Technician Fifth Grade Leonard N. Miller and his contributions to our state and country; therefore, be it Resolved by the Legislature of West Virginia:That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to name bridge number 44-010/00-001.09 (44A024), locally known as MT OLIVE BRIDGE (SCBB), beginning at Latitude 38.815807, Longitude -81.430185, ending at Latitude 38.815550, Longitude -81.430992, carrying Reedyville Road, CR 10, over Left Fork Reedy Creek in Roane County, the "U. S. Army Technician Fifth Grade Leonard N. Miller Memorial Bridge"; and, be it Further Resolved, That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to have made and be placed signs identifying the bridge as the "U. S. Army Technician Fifth Grade Leonard N. Miller Memorial Bridge"; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk of the House is hereby directed to forward a copy of this resolution to the Commissioner of the Division of Highways.
