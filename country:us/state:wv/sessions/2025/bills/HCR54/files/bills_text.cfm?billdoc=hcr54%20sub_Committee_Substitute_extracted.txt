Red = new code to be enacted Committee SubstituteforHOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 54(By Delegate Sheedy)[Originating in the Committee on Energy and Public Works; Reported on March 13, 2025] Requesting the Division of Highways name bridge number 52-009/00.004.54(52A159) at Latitude/Longitude 39.70766, -80.53262, locally known as Sugar Run Bridge carrying CR 09 over West Virginia Fork of Fish Creek in Wetzel County, the "U.S. Army PFC Howard Bissett Memorial Bridge".Whereas, Howard Bissett was born to Ezra and Mary Blanche Bissett on August 23, 1930; andWhereas, after PFC Bissett left school, he worked on his family's farm for four years before he joined the Army; andWhereas, on January 17, 1952, Howard Bissett joined the Army; andWhereas, PFC Howard Bissett served in the United States Army as a Supply Handler for eight years and earned the National Defense Service Medal; andWhereas, He married Juanita Hawkins in 1954 and together they had Richard, Deborah, and Annette, who eventually gave PFC Bissett and Juanita ten grandchildren and fourteen great-grandchildren; andWhereas, PFC Bissett was a faithful member of Littleton Church of God and he worked for Penn Line Service as a grounds person until he retired; andWhereas, PFC Bissett died on March 6, 2013, at his home; andWhereas, It is fitting that an enduring memorial be established to commemorate Private First Class Howard Bissett and his contributions to our state and country; therefore, be it Resolved by the Legislature of West Virginia:That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to name bridge number 52-009/00.004.54(52A159) at Latitude/Longitude 39.70766, -80.53262, locally known as Sugar Run Bridge, carrying CR 09 over West Virginia Fork of Fish Creek in Wetzel County, the "U.S. Army PFC Howard Bissett Memorial Bridge"; and, be it Further Resolved, That the Division of Highways is hereby requested to have made and be placed signs identifying the bridge as the "U.S. Army PFC Howard Bissett Memorial Bridge"; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk of the House forward a copy of this resolution to the Commissioner of the Division of Highways.
