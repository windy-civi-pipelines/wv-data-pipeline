Red = new code to be enacted WEST VIRGINIA LEGISLATURE2025 REGULAR SESSIONIntroducedHouse Bill 3302By Delegates Roop, Cooper, Dean, Bridges, Pritt, and Brooks[Introduced March 11, 2025; referred to the Committee on Government Organization]A BILL to amend the Code of West Virginia, 1931, by adding a new section, designated §9-5-34, relating to requirements for all entities that contract with the State of West Virginia to distribute funds for homeless shelters.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: article 5. Miscellaneous provisions. §9-5-34. Contracts for distributions to homeless shelters. All entities who contract with the State of West Virginia to administer and distribute funds for homeless shelters shall have a board of directors consisting of one representative from each region of West Virginia. NOTE: The purpose of this bill is to require entities that contract with the State of West Virginia to distribute funds for homeless shelters to have a board of directors with a member of that board representing each region of the state.Strike-throughs indicate language that would be stricken from a heading or the present law and underscoring indicates new language that would be added.
